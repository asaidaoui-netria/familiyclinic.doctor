Three Secrets to Becoming an Active Participant in Your Health... and Your Life

Many of you are looking for ways to take back control of your health, well-being, and life. Here are three fundamental truths, drawn from clinical practice and neuroscience, to guide you in this quest for autonomy and healing.

Secret #1: The Therapeutic Relationship Heals... Biologically

When you feel heard, understood, and safe with your doctor or therapist, your body responds. This human connection activates hormones like oxytocin and endorphins, which:

  • Soothe the nervous system
  • Reduce inflammation
  • Strengthen immunity
  • Promote cellular repair

This is not "just psychological": the connection heals.

Secret #2: Don't Treat the Symptom, Seek the Cause

Pain, fatigue, digestive disorders... These are not enemies, but signals. What matters is the terrain, the origin, the story behind the symptom.

Conventional medicine tends to divide the body into isolated organs. But you are a whole: a body, a mind, a history, a sensitivity. Healing begins when we reconnect the physical, emotional, and relational dimensions.

Secret #3: The Bond of Trust is Your First Medicine

If you feel free to speak, to be yourself, to express your doubts without judgment... If your doctor listens to you with attention and kindness... Then you are already on the path to healing.

But the rest belongs to you:

  • Inform yourself from reliable sources
  • Ask questions
  • Adopt habits that make you feel good
  • Track your progress
