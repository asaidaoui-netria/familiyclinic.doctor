How to Close Intestinal "Holes" in 21 Days

Intestinal Barrier Repair Program

A systematic program to repair intestinal barrier in three phases: cleanse & soothe, nourish & repair, strengthen & stabilize. Learn how to heal intestinal permeability in 21 days.

Phase 1: Cleanse & Soothe (Days 1-7)

Objective: Eliminate irritants, reduce inflammation, initiate microbiome rebalancing.

❌ Elimination: gluten, dairy products, refined sugars, alcohol, tobacco, additives

✅ Anti-inflammatory foods: turmeric, ginger, olive oil, sardines, cooked vegetables

  • Restorative teas: chamomile, lemon balm, ginger, green tea
  • Gentle saline cure (warm water + non-iodized salt + lemon) on empty stomach 1x/day
  • Abdominal breathing & heart coherence 5 min morning/evening

Phase 2: Nourish & Repair (Days 8-14)

Objective: Strengthen the intestinal mucosa and nourish the good bacteria.

  • Homemade broths (chicken, vegetables, ginger) rich in glutamine
  • Gentle fibers: sweet potato, zucchini, carrot, banana, gluten-free oats
  • Natural probiotics: kefir, sauerkraut, enriched plant yogurt
  • Supplements (if validated by a professional): glutamine, zinc, quercetin, omega-3
  • Daily follow-up via interactive form (symptoms, transit, energy)

Phase 3: Strengthen & Stabilize (Days 15-21)

Objective: Consolidate the intestinal barrier and install a durable routine.

  • Prebiotic-rich menus: garlic, onion, leek, Jerusalem artichoke, artichoke
  • Homemade green juices (celery, cucumber, apple, ginger)
  • Gentle physical activity: walking, yoga, stretching
  • Education: brochure on microbiome and healing foods
  • Final assessment: transit, energy, mood, perceived inflammation

Professional Support

This program provides a systematic approach to healing intestinal "holes" in 21 days. For personalized guidance and monitoring, consult with a qualified healthcare professional who can adapt the recommendations to your specific needs.
