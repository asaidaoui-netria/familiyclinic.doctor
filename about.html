Dr. Said-Alaoui Moulay Abdellah

Family Medicine Physician

Dr. Said-Alaoui Moulay Abdellah is a former military physician with over 40 years of experience at all levels of medical support, both national and international. As a family physician, he focuses on preventing lifestyle-related diseases. His experience with patients has given him sufficient perspective to understand the multitude of health problems that modern medicine still faces.

By focusing on the nutritional and environmental causes of disease and what has become nutritional therapy, his approach is to provide patients, both healthy and sick, with clear scientific messages. He seeks to expand his patients' knowledge by helping them understand the causes of their illnesses. The nutritional advice he provides is thus easier to follow, and patients regularly report impressive and often rapid results.

In doing so, he opens new perspectives for patients by powerfully contributing to what Hippocrates declared: "Let food be your medicine before your medicines become your food." Although questioning eating habits can sometimes be difficult to follow, patients respond positively to the natural care offered, whether preventive for healthy people or curative for people suffering from chronic diseases.

By eliminating toxic and disease-causing foods, within a few days, life resumes its course, and it is precisely the attentive follow-up that often produces unexpected results.
